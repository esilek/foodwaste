    Our mission:

    Saving the planet one meal at a time through reducing food wastage and generating a hub for planet-friendly converts


    We’re building a community space for nourished eaters and flourishing environments. We aim to educate and inspire healthy and stustainable eating by connecting you to great projects and top tips. We strive for food system change through drawing together networks of people and organizations to generate environmentally, socially, and economically sustainable ways of alleviating hunger and obesity.
